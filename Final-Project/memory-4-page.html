WEST COAST FEVER DREAM

For every winter break, spring break, and summer break, I never really traveled anywhere. I've always gone home. However, for my last spring break, I wanted to visit the one and only, Summer! As a super senior, many of my 'non-archi' friends already graduated and began their chapter of adulthood. Because she was currently based in San Diego, CA, we both thought that it would be so much fun to be together again like old times! From La Jolla Cove, UCSD, sand dunes, Balboa Park, Downtown San Diego, and DisneyLand, the memories of my time there with Summer are truly unforgettable.

FIRST STOP, SEATTLE!

My family decided to travel to Seattle, Washington during the summer of 2018. That summer was quite special. Everyone in the Kim Family was full of joy and excitement. I had just graduated high school and I was looking forward to a new chapter of my life - Carnegie Mellon University. My older brother had also achieved his goal to intern at Amazon, which opened up his chapter in Seattle.

My parents, who have never taken a day off from work, wanted to celebrate and go on a little family vacation for once. With my brother located in Seattle for the summer, they thought it was the perfect opportunity to explore a new city. However, it wasn't simply a little family vacation to me. My parents constantly work hard to support me and my brother, which is why they never take days off or go on vacation and travel. Once I can support myself in the future, I hope to create more memories like this with my family.

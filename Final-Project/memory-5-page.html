S23 CARNIVAL

The last time CMU's Spring Carnival had perfect weather was during my freshmen year. In a way, it felt like mother nature was gifting us a little graduation gift to the graduating class of 2023. After being with the same group of people for the last 5 years, this carnival felt special for each and every one of us. From riding a few carnival rides, checking out each booth, admiring the beauty inside Phipps, laying down in the sun at Schenley, and taking a stroll to Panther Hollow, it was quite the 4-day weekend.

NOIR ET BLANC

This was quite an adventure. In French, "Noir et Blanc" translates to "Black and White". After completing a black and white film photography course in the previous semester, I fell in love with the concept and beauty of film photography. As it was the year of the pandemic and our lock-down, I had the time to dive back into a lot of my hobbies. Although our third year was completely remote, my friends and I remained at our apartment in Pittsburgh. Staying inside every single day can become frustrating, so we decided to go to the Strip District! We had just begun classes for the fall 2020 semester, which meant we had a bit of time before school work got crazy. From Roxanne's Dried Flowers to Peace, Love, and Little Donuts, we were so tired by the end of the day.
